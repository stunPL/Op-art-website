What is op art

Op-art or visualism - a trend in graphics, fashion, applied arts and painting, the task of which is to influence the viewer's eye, not his intellect or emotions; using abstract combinations of lines giving geometric optical illusions, lighting, dynamic and textural effects, aimed at creating the impression of depth and movement by vibrating the field of view. The 1950s and 1960s are considered to be the peak period of op-art.
